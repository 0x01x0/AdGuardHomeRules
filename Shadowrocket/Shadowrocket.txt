拦截模式选择自定IP
DNSSEC和EDNS开启

比如
A拦截到6.6.6.6
AAAA拦截到::0606:0606

常用的本地链路有
127.0.0.1 localhost
127.0.0.1 localhost.localdomain
127.0.0.1 local
255.255.255.255 broadcasthost
::1 localhost
::1 ip6-localhost
::1 ip6-loopback
fe80::1%lo0 localhost
ff00::0 ip6-localnet
ff00::0 ip6-mcastprefix
ff02::1 ip6-allnodes
ff02::2 ip6-allrouters
ff02::3 ip6-allhosts
0.0.0.0 0.0.0.0

只有ALL合集需要调整，补充集无需调整，原版即可
